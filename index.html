      About Blue

      A small community tool to help you find clean water stations during Mardi Gras.

      Blue is built as a lightweight static app so anyone can open it from a phone without installing anything. Use it to share water sources, help others stay hydrated, and keep the party going safely.

      • Map-based station directory with live markers
      • Quick sharing UI for reporting new clean water spots
      • Designed to work on mobile with a responsive layout
